# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is AI Resume Builder?

AI Resume Builder is a modern web application that helps you create professional, ATS-optimized resumes using AI-powered content generation. It offers multiple templates, smart suggestions, and export options.

### Is it free to use?

Yes, the application is open-source and free to use. You can self-host it or use a deployed version.

### What makes this different from other resume builders?

- **AI-Powered**: Uses Google Gemini AI for intelligent content generation
- **ATS-Optimized**: Ensures your resume passes Applicant Tracking Systems
- **Modern Tech Stack**: Built with Next.js 16 and React 19
- **Multiple Export Formats**: PDF and DOCX downloads
- **Open Source**: Free to use and customize

## AI Features

### What AI features are available?

- **Professional Summary Generation**
- **Work Experience Bullet Points**
- **ATS Score Analysis**
- **Cover Letter Generation**
- **Skill Suggestions**
- **Resume Improvement Tips**

### How accurate is the AI?

The AI provides high-quality suggestions based on best practices, but you should always:
- Review generated content
- Personalize it to your experience
- Ensure accuracy

### Are there limits on AI usage?

Yes, to ensure fair usage:
- **Limit**: 10 AI requests per minute
- **Cooldown**: 60 seconds if exceeded

### Can I edit AI-generated content?

Absolutely! AI content is a starting point. You can and should edit it to match your experience and style.

### What if AI generates incorrect information?

Always review and edit AI-generated content. The AI doesn't know your specific experience, so personalization is essential.

## Exporting & Sharing

### What export formats are supported?

- **PDF**: Print-ready, ATS-compatible
- **DOCX**: Editable in Microsoft Word/Google Docs

### Is the PDF ATS-compatible?

Yes! Our PDFs are optimized for Applicant Tracking Systems:
- No images or graphics
- Standard fonts
- Proper text structure
- Searchable text

### Can I share my resume with others?

Yes! Click "Share" to generate a shareable link that:
- Works without login
- Is view-only
- Expires after 30 days (default)

### How do I download my resume?

1. Open your resume
2. Click "Download"
3. Choose PDF or DOCX
4. File downloads automatically

## Troubleshooting

### My resume isn't saving

**Solutions**:
1. Check internet connection
2. Refresh the page
3. Sign out and sign in again

### AI features aren't working

**Solutions**:
1. Check if you've exceeded rate limit (wait 1 minute)
2. Ensure you've provided enough context
3. Try again

### PDF export isn't working

**Solutions**:
1. Disable pop-up blocker
2. Try a different browser
3. Ensure resume has content

## Best Practices

### How can I make my resume stand out?

1. **Use AI wisely**: Generate content, then personalize
2. **Quantify achievements**: Include numbers and metrics
3. **Tailor to job**: Customize for each application
4. **Keep it concise**: 1-2 pages maximum
5. **Proofread**: Check for errors

### What should I avoid?

- ❌ Generic summaries
- ❌ Spelling/grammar errors
- ❌ Irrelevant information
- ❌ Unprofessional email addresses
- ❌ Photos (unless required)

### How often should I update my resume?

- After major achievements
- When changing jobs
- Every 6 months minimum
- Before job applications
