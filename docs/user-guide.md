## Creating Your First Resume

### Step 1: Create a New Resume

1. Click the "**+ New Resume**" button
2. Enter a title (e.g., "Software Engineer Resume")
3. Click "Create"

### Step 2: Add Personal Information

1. Fill in your basic details:
   - First Name and Last Name
   - Job Title
   - Email and Phone
   - Address (optional)
   - LinkedIn URL (optional)
   - Website/Portfolio (optional)

2. Click "Save" to update

### Step 3: Add Professional Summary

**Option A: Write Your Own**
1. Type your professional summary in the text box
2. Keep it concise (3-4 sentences)
3. Highlight key achievements

**Option B: Use AI Generation** ‚ú®
1. Click "**Generate with AI**"
2. Enter your job title
3. Add years of experience
4. List key skills
5. Click "Generate"
6. Review and edit the AI-generated summary

### Step 4: Add Work Experience

1. Click "**+ Add Experience**"
2. Fill in the details:
   - Job Title
   - Company Name
   - Location (City, State)
   - Start Date and End Date
   - Check "Currently Working" if applicable

3. Add work responsibilities:
   - **Manual**: Type bullet points
   - **AI-Powered**: Click "Generate Bullets" for AI suggestions

**AI Bullet Points Tips**:
- Provide context about your role
- Mention technologies used
- Include achievements if possible

### Step 5: Add Education

1. Click "**+ Add Education**"
2. Fill in:
   - University/School Name
   - Degree (e.g., Bachelor of Science)
   - Major/Field of Study
   - Start and End Date
   - Description (optional)

### Step 6: Add Skills

1. Click "**+ Add Skill**"
2. Enter skill name
3. Optionally set proficiency level

**AI Skill Suggestions** ‚ú®:
1. Click "**Suggest Skills**"
2. Enter your job title
3. Review AI-suggested skills
4. Add relevant ones to your resume

## Using AI Features

### 1. AI Summary Generation

**Purpose**: Create a compelling professional summary

**How to Use**:
1. Go to Summary section
2. Click "Generate with AI"
3. Provide:
   - Target job title
   - Years of experience
   - Key skills
4. Click "Generate"
5. Edit as needed

**Example Input**:
- Job Title: "Full Stack Developer"
- Experience: "5 years building web applications"
- Skills: "React, Node.js, MongoDB, AWS"

### 2. AI Bullet Point Generation

**Purpose**: Create impactful work experience descriptions

**How to Use**:
1. In Experience section, click "Generate Bullets"
2. Provide context:
   - What you did
   - Technologies used
   - Team size (if applicable)
3. Click "Generate"
4. Select bullets you like

**Tips for Better Results**:
- Be specific about your role
- Mention quantifiable achievements
- Include relevant technologies

### 3. ATS Score Analysis

**Purpose**: Optimize resume for Applicant Tracking Systems

**How to Use**:
1. Complete your resume
2. Click "**Check ATS Score**"
3. Optionally paste job description
4. Review score and suggestions
5. Implement recommendations

**What to Look For**:
- Score above 80% is excellent
- Follow keyword suggestions
- Ensure proper formatting

### 4. Cover Letter Generation

**Purpose**: Create personalized cover letters

**How to Use**:
1. Go to AI Tools
2. Select "Cover Letter Generator"
3. Enter:
   - Job title
   - Company name
   - Job description (optional)
4. Click "Generate"
5. Copy and customize

### 5. Resume Improvement Suggestions

**Purpose**: Get AI feedback on your resume

**How to Use**:
1. Click "**Improve Resume**"
2. Optionally specify target job
3. Review AI suggestions
4. Implement changes

## Exporting Your Resume

### Export to PDF

1. Click "**Download**" button
2. Select "**Download as PDF**"
3. Wait for generation
4. PDF downloads automatically

**PDF Features**:
- High-quality rendering
- Print-ready
- ATS-compatible
- Preserves formatting

### Export to DOCX

1. Click "**Download**" button
2. Select "**Download as DOCX**"
3. File downloads instantly

**DOCX Benefits**:
- Editable in Microsoft Word
- Compatible with Google Docs
- Easy to customize further

### Share Your Resume

1. Click "**Share**" button
2. Copy the generated link
3. Share via:
   - Email
   - Social media
   - Messaging apps

**Share Link Features**:
- View-only access
- No login required
- Expires after 30 days (default)
- Track views (coming soon)

## Tips for Best Results

### Writing Effective Content

**Professional Summary**:
- ‚úÖ Start with your title and years of experience
- ‚úÖ Highlight 2-3 key achievements
- ‚úÖ Mention relevant skills
- ‚ùå Don't write in first person ("I am...")
- ‚ùå Don't exceed 4-5 lines

**Work Experience**:
- ‚úÖ Use action verbs (Led, Developed, Implemented)
- ‚úÖ Include quantifiable results (increased by 40%)
- ‚úÖ Focus on achievements, not just duties
- ‚ùå Don't use passive voice
- ‚ùå Don't include irrelevant details

**Skills**:
- ‚úÖ List relevant technical skills
- ‚úÖ Include soft skills
- ‚úÖ Match job requirements
- ‚ùå Don't list outdated technologies
- ‚ùå Don't overstate proficiency

### ATS Optimization

1. **Use Standard Section Headings**:
   - "Work Experience" not "Career Journey"
   - "Education" not "Academic Background"

2. **Include Keywords**:
   - From job description
   - Industry-specific terms
   - Relevant technologies

3. **Avoid**:
   - Tables and columns
   - Images and graphics
   - Unusual fonts
   - Headers and footers

### Design Best Practices

1. **Keep It Clean**:
   - Use plenty of white space
   - Consistent formatting
   - Clear section breaks

2. **Choose Readable Fonts**:
   - Stick to professional fonts
   - Use appropriate sizes
   - Maintain consistency

3. **Limit to 1-2 Pages**:
   - One page for <10 years experience
   - Two pages for extensive experience
   - Never exceed two pages

### Using AI Effectively

1. **Provide Context**:
   - More details = better results
   - Include specific achievements
   - Mention technologies used

2. **Review and Edit**:
   - AI is a starting point
   - Personalize the content
   - Ensure accuracy

3. **Iterate**:
   - Try different prompts
   - Combine multiple generations
   - Refine until satisfied

## Troubleshooting

### Resume Not Saving

**Solution**:
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Try again

### AI Not Generating Content

**Solution**:
- Ensure you've provided enough context
- Check rate limit (10 requests/minute)
- Wait a moment and try again

### Export Not Working

**Solution**:
- Disable pop-up blocker
- Try different browser
- Contact support
